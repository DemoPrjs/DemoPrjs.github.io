Payment Workflow

End-to-end VR payment process

From counselor-initiated request through fiscal approval queues, automated validation against authorization, rates, and available budget — with explicit error and correction handling at every step.

Payment Lifecycle

  1. 1
    Request InitiationCounselor submits a payment request against an active authorization line item with vendor invoice or receipt attached.
  2. 2
    Automated ValidationSystem validates authorization status, approved service code, vendor rate, available budget, and supporting documentation.
  3. 3
    Fiscal Approval QueueRouted by tier — Fiscal Tier 1 for routine, Tier 2 for high-value or exception items, with SLA timers visible to reviewers.
  4. 4
    Error & Correction HandlingFailed validations return to counselor with itemized errors; corrections preserve audit history and re-enter the queue.
  5. 5
    DisbursementApproved payments transmitted to State accounting / bank; payment status returns and posts to consumer ledger.
  6. 6
    Post-PaymentVoids, refunds, and reversals trigger automatic encumbrance restoration and ledger adjustments.
