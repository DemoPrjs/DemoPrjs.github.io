Case Flow & Operator Lifecycle

BEP-specific case flow with VR coordination

Captures certification & training history, supports vendor and facility/site agreements, required documentation uploads, and CEUs — with seamless funds coordination for clients who originally entered through VR.

BEP-specific case flow (non-VR)

  1. 1
    Intake & EligibilityDirect BEP intake for legally blind applicants, with eligibility verification independent of VR caseload.
  2. 2
    Training EnrollmentPre-licensure training program with attendance, evaluations, and curriculum modules captured in the case record.
  3. 3
    Certification & LicensureIssue BEP license; track certification history, retests, and continuing education units (CEUs).
  4. 4
    Operator AgreementsUpload signed vendor agreements, facility/site agreements, insurance, and W-9 — with e-signature.
  5. 5
    Assignment & OperationFacility placement, monthly reporting, performance reviews, and CEU tracking through retirement or exit.

Operator selection, assignment & exit

  1. A
    Operator SelectionEligible operators are ranked by seniority, performance, and training scores when a facility becomes available.
  2. B
    Facility AssignmentAssign operator to one or more vending sites or cafeterias; system enforces conflicts and capacity rules.
  3. C
    ReassignmentMove operator between facilities with full transition checklist (inventory transfer, deposit reconciliation, key handover).
  4. D
    Exit from BEPCapture exit reason (retirement, voluntary, performance), final reports, and asset return; record retained for audit.

🔁 Coordinating funds & case history between VR and BEP

When a client originally entered through Vocational Rehabilitation (VR), their case history is preserved and accessible to BEP. Funds may be coordinated across programs for allowable BEP-related purchases (e.g., adaptive technology, training tools) with full auditability and approval workflows.

What the integration delivers

  • ✓
    Vendor payrollRun payroll for operator’s employees through QuickBooks with hours and earnings imported from the BEP platform.
  • ✓
    Tax processingFederal & state withholdings, 1099 generation, and year-end tax forms aligned to BEP earnings records.
  • ✓
    Set-aside paymentsCalculated automatically from gross revenue and remitted to GVRA via ACH on a scheduled cadence.
  • ✓
    Seed Live telemetryCloud-based device management feed of vending sales, ingested in near real time and sorted by facility location and by operator — including operators with multiple sites.
  • ✓
    Multi-location roll-upOperators see consolidated sales across all assigned facilities; GVRA reports drill from operator → facility → device.
  • ✓
    ReconciliationTelemetry sales, bank deposits, invoices, and journal entries reconciled back to facility & operator records.
